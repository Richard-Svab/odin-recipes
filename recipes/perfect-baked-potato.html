Perfect Baked Potato

Description

This baked potato has a crisp, golden skin, and is light and fluffy on the inside - a perfect baked potato. Serve with a dollop of sour cream if you like. Great comfort food!

Ingredients

  • medium potato
  • teaspoon olive oil
  • half teaspoon salt
  • pinch of black pepper
  • quarter cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 150 Celsius
  2. Scrub the potato and pierce skin with knife
  3. Rub it with olive oil and then with salt
  4. Place the potato in the oven and bake for 90 minutes
  5. Slice the potato down to centre
  6. Sprinkle shredded cheese over top
  7. Serve it with butter and black pepper
